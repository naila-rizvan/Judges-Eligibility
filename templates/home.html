District 116 Judges Eligibility Checker

Welcome to the Toastmasters District 116 Judges Eligibility Checker! This web application helps Contest Managers at Division and District levels to automatically determine whether a judge is eligible to judge contestants based on the latest verified membership data.

Admins can upload the master membership file, and Contest Managers or members can quickly check judge eligibility. This eliminates manual verification, prevents errors, and ensures compliance with Toastmasters International contest rules.
